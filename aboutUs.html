BUILDING STRATEGISTS

A student-powered consulting and strategy society.

180 Degrees Consulting ARSD is a community of driven problem-solvers committed to developing future leaders through real consulting engagements, strategy projects, and structured professional growth.

WHO WE ARE

180 Degrees Consulting, Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College, is a student-led social impact consulting body established in 2024.

Engage

We foster rigorous discourse through research forums, stakeholder dialogues, and curated knowledge exchanges, cultivating analytical depth and sectoral perspective.

Apply-in-Action

Members work on live consulting mandates for non-profits and social enterprises, applying research frameworks, data-led insights, and strategic tools to design implementable, outcome-oriented solutions.

Impact Beyond Campus

Our engagements strengthen mission-driven organizations while preparing students for leadership through practical exposure, professional mentorship, and measurable contributions to scalable, long-term social impact.

OUR FLAGSHIP EVENTS

Vision

We envision an equitable, sustainable future where impact-driven organizations thrive without constraints of expertise or resources. 180 Degrees Consulting, ARSD aspires to lead social impact consulting by developing responsible leaders and enabling meaningful solutions to critical global and national challenges.

Mission

We empower non-profits and social enterprises with accessible, research-driven consulting, leveraging student talent and professional mentorship to create scalable, accountable, and sustainable social impact.
