Learning Beyond Classrooms

SHAPING MINDS

Workshops, competitions, and industry interactions.

Our events bring students closer to the world of consulting and strategy through case competitions, speaker sessions, workshops, and experiential learning.

Odyssey 180'25

Odyssey 180, the flagship two-day conclave by 180 Degrees Consulting ARSD, delivered an immersive, high-impact experience integrating expert-led insights, a rigorous real-world case competition, and a vibrant networking mixer. The event cultivated strategic acumen, sharpened problem-solving capabilities, and forged meaningful professional connections, empowering students with industry exposure and future-ready competencies.

Synapse'25

Synapse 2025, the intra-college case competition by 180 Degrees Consulting ARSD, served as a high-intensity problem-solving arena where students tackled complex business scenarios. The initiative sharpened analytical judgement, encouraged innovative thinking, and nurtured collaborative leadership, enabling participants to translate classroom learning into structured, impact-driven solutions.
