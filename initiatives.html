Consult Sphere

Get ready to elevate your career trajectory with the launch of Consult Sphere 2025. Presented by 180 Degrees Consulting ARSD, this premier initiative is specifically designed for non-ARSD students who are driven to master the intricacies of strategy and social impact. It is more than just a program; it is a gateway to the professional consulting world, where your ideas meet real-world challenges.

As an Associate Consultant, you will dive into a live client-based project, moving beyond theory to deliver measurable results. To ensure you are industry-ready, the journey begins with an intensive training and workshop week featuring experts from MBB and Big 4 firms. You will master essential hard skills like Excel and strategic frameworks while refining soft skills like executive presence and communication. Participants also gain access to premium Udemy courses and will earn Internship Certificates from both the client and 180DC ARSD. The selection process is rigorous—consisting of an application, a mock case, and a personal interview—ensuring a cohort of the most dedicated and curious minds.
